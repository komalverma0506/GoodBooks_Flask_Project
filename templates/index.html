Get Started

We believe in the power of the written and spoken words

You may be a reader or a writer. You may be looking for a pleasurable escape or to improve your life personally or professionally. No matter what your goals are, we support you.

We envision a future where all have the reading skills necessary to reach their full potential. Our mission is to help everyone to become lifelong readers by empowering communities to provide individualized instruction with measurable results.

  • We fundamentally believe that the ability to read transforms lives
  • We seize opportunities to make a positive impact, and are relentless in the pursuit of our mission and goals
  • We mobilize and intentionally nurture a community of members, volunteers, and partners to make a difference.

We believe that everyone has a part to play in working to close the opportunity gap, and that collaborative efforts create unlimited potential for students and communities. We maximize impact through collective leadership and build meaningful partnerships to advance our shared vision and strategy.
